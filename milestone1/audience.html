Where Do Players Live?

The primary audience for Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) is based in North America and Europe, especially in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. However, the game has a growing global presence, with communities in Australia, parts of Asia, and other regions.

Age Demographics

Most D&D players are between 18 and 34 years old. There is also a significant number of teenage players, as well as adults in their 30s and 40s who have played for many years.

Gender Distribution

Historically, D&D had more male players, but recent surveys show the audience is becoming more balanced. Currently, about 60% of players are male and 40% are female, with increasing participation from non-binary and gender-diverse players.

Common Hobbies

  • Reading fantasy and science fiction books
  • Playing video games and board games
  • Writing stories or creating art
  • Cosplay and attending conventions
  • Watching fantasy movies and TV shows
